About me

Welcome to my programming journey! I am an Applied Informatics graduate passionate about building innovative web applications. I graduated from the Faculty of Applied Informatics at the University of Macedonia with an average of 7.3/10 and completed a six-month internship at the Pfizer Center of Digital Innovation.
